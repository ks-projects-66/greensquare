The Decision Frame

 GreenSquare · greensquare.ai
1

The decision

What exactly are you deciding, and why now? What happens if you do nothing?

2

The real question

Is the decision you have been handed the actual decision? What harder question might it be standing in front of?

3

The options

What are the real options, including doing nothing and doing something else entirely? On which two or three criteria will this actually be decided?

4

Facts against assumptions

What do you know, and what are you assuming? Mark which is which. Which one or two assumptions does the answer most depend on?

5

The recommendation

Given the above, what is the call? Name the single assumption that, if wrong, would flip it.

6

Next steps

What are the first concrete steps, who owns each, and by when?

7

The risks that would change the call

What would have to be true for this to be wrong? What is the earliest signal you would see it?

Work top to bottom. The honest answer to question 2 is usually where the value is. © 2026 GreenSquare
