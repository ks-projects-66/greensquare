The output

A five-part brief, written in the conversation.

You load one file, describe the decision and answer a few focused questions. The advisor then produces a tight Decision Brief in markdown, built around what the call actually turns on.

  • The decision, and why it matters now
  • The options, compared against the same criteria
  • The recommendation, with the assumptions exposed
  • The next steps, concrete enough to act on
  • The risks or unknowns that would change the call

The brief is the product. It is designed to help you decide, explain the logic and defend the recommendation.

How it works

Three steps, no setup.

01

Load one file

Paste the Decision Brief instruction set into a new chat, or upload it as the first file.

02

Describe the decision

Use your own words. The advisor decides whether it is a real decision and asks the questions needed to make it rigorous.

03

Answer, then decide

The advisor challenges vague inputs, tests the framing and produces the brief once the facts are specific enough.
